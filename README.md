# BA Toolbox

A practical reference toolkit for Business Analysts and Requirements Engineers working on IT projects.

---

## What Is the BA Toolbox?

The BA Toolbox is a curated collection of guides, matrices, schemas, and templates designed to help Business Analysts (BAs) work more effectively — whether you're joining a new project, choosing the right method for a problem, producing high-quality deliverables, or leveraging AI to accelerate your work.

It is structured around the full BA lifecycle: from understanding a problem and engaging stakeholders, through eliciting and prioritising requirements, all the way to iterative delivery and lessons learned.

## Who Is This For?

- **Business Analysts** looking for a structured approach to a new project or a challenging situation
- **Requirements Engineers** needing a quick reference for methods and deliverables
- **Product Owners and teams** wanting to understand what BA artifacts to expect and why they matter
- **Anyone new to BA work** who needs a practical, phase-by-phase guide to get started

## How to Use the Toolbox

1. **New to a project?** → Start with [`BA_New_Joiner_Guide.md`](BA_New_Joiner_Guide.md)
2. **Have a specific problem?** → Use [`BA_Methods_Matrix.md`](BA_Methods_Matrix.md) to find the right method
3. **Need to produce a deliverable?** → Check [`BA_Deliverables_Matrix.md`](BA_Deliverables_Matrix.md) or [`AI_Enabled_BA_Deliverables_Catalog.md`](AI_Enabled_BA_Deliverables_Catalog.md)
4. **Want a standard format for your artifacts?** → See [`BA_Context_Block_Schema.md`](BA_Context_Block_Schema.md) and the [`Context_block_examples/`](Context_block_examples/) folder

## Key Principles

- **Problem-first thinking** — always understand the problem before jumping to solutions
- **Living documents** — these artifacts evolve as projects progress; update them regularly
- **AI-augmented, not AI-replaced** — AI tools accelerate BA work; human judgement, stakeholder trust, and domain understanding remain essential
- **Standards-aligned** — methods and terminology are grounded in IREB, BABOK, Agile/Scrum, SAFe, and Design Thinking
